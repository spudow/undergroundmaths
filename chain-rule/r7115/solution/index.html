When does this cone have a maximum volume?

Solution

A hollow container is to be made out of a fixed total area πa2πa2 of sheet metal and its shape is to be that of a right circular cone completed by the circular base on which it stands. Find the radius of the base when the container encloses maximum volume.

The surface area AA of a cone with radius rr and height hh is given by A=πr2+πsrA=πr2+πsr, where s=h2+r2⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯√s=h2+r2 is the sloped side of the cone.

The πr2πr2 part comes from the base of the cone. The other part when laid flat is a sector with radius ss and arclength 2πr2πr.

If the angle in the sector is θθ, then sθ=2πrsθ=2πr. The area of the sector is 12s2θ12s2θ, which on eliminating θθ is πsrπsr, as above.
