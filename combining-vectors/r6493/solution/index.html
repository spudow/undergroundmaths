Combining Vectors
Review question

If ai+bj+ckai+bj+ck is perpendicular to these vectors, what is a:b:ca:b:c?

Solution

Given that the vector ai+bj+ckai+bj+ck is perpendicular to each of the vectors 5i+3j−4k5i+3j−4k and 2i−j−6k2i−j−6k, find the ratios a:b:ca:b:c.

Two vectors are perpendicular exactly if their scalar product is equal to zero.

So we know that (ai+bj+ck).(5i+3j−4k)=0(ai+bj+ck).(5i+3j−4k)=0 and (ai+bj+ck).(2i−j−6k)=0(ai+bj+ck).(2i−j−6k)=0.
