Counting & Binomials
Review question

Which dates contain no repetitions of a digit?

Ref: R5212

Solution

This question concerns calendar dates of the form

d1d2/m1m2/y1y2y3y4d1d2/m1m2/y1y2y3y4
in the order day/month/year.

The question specifically concerns those dates which contain no repetitions of a digit. For example, the date 23/05/196723/05/1967 is such date but 07/12/197407/12/1974 is not such a date as both 1=m1=y11=m1=y1 and 7=d2=y37=d2=y3 are repeated digits.

We will use the Gregorian Calendar throughout (this is the calendar system that is standard throughout most of the world; see below.)

  1. Show that there is no date with no repetition of digits in the years from 20002000 to 20992099.

Any date which has a year from 20002000 to 20992099 will have y1=2y1=2 and y2=0y2=0.

Therefore, m1m1 cannot be 00, so m1=1m1=1.

Since there are only 1212 months, m2m2 can be only 00, 11 or 22, but any of these values will lead to repetitions.

Hence, there is no date with no repetition of digits in the years from 20002000 to 20992099.

  1. What was the last date before today, 03/11/201003/11/2010, with no repetition of digits? Explain your answer.

We should aim for dates of the form d1d2/m1m2/19y3y4d1d2/m1m2/19y3y4, since we already know that there is no date from 20002000 to 20992099 that will work.

Also, since we want the last day, we should find the biggest values possible, starting with the year. The greatest value for a year with no repeated digits is 19871987.

Now we aim to find the greatest possible value for the month. Since 1010,1111 and 1212 will all lead to repeated digit 11, this is 0606.

For the day, we cannot use 00 and 11 and so the day cannot be 3030 or 3131. So the biggest day with no repeated values is 2525.

Hence the last date before 03/11/201003/11/2010 with no repetition of digits is 25/06/198725/06/1987.

  1. When will the next such date be? Explain your answer.

To find the next such date after 03/11/201003/11/2010 we aim to choose the smallest values for day, year and month, starting with the year.

Since every date from 20002000 to 20992099 repeats digits, the date we seek should be of the form d1d2/m1m2/2y2y3y4d1d2/m1m2/2y2y3y4, with y2>0y2>0.

If y2=1y2=1, then, in order to avoid repetitions, m1=0m1=0 and d1=3d1=3.

But then we will have no possible value for d2d2, as we cannot use 00 or 11.

So y2y2 cannot be 11. Moreover, by the same argument, y3y3 and y4y4 should also be different from 11.

The next possible value is y2=3y2=3. Since we want the minimum value of y1y2y3y4y1y2y3y4, and we cannot use 11 or 00, y1y2y3y4=2345y1y2y3y4=2345.

So the minimum value of the month that we can choose is 0606, and the minimum one for the day is 1717.

Therefore the next date with no repeated digits is 17/06/234517/06/2345.

  1. How many such dates were there in years from 19001900 to 19991999? Explain your answer.

Since we want years from 19001900 to 19991999, y1=1y1=1 and y2=9y2=9.

Also, m1=0m1=0, as it cannot be 11. Since we have used 11 and 00, and the only days starting with 33 are 3030 and 3131, which cannot be used, d1=2d1=2.

For the other positions, namely, d2d2, m2m2, y3y3 and y4y4, we can choose any of the 66 digits that remain unused.

So we have 66 ways of choosing d2d2, 55 ways of choosing m2m2 (as it should be different from d2d2), 44 ways of choosing y3y3 and 33 ways of choosing y4y4.

Hence, there are 6×5×4×3=3606×5×4×3=360 such dates in the years from 19001900 to 19991999.
