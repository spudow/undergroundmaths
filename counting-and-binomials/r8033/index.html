Counting & Binomials
Review question

Can we find a sequence of solutions to the equation x2−2y2=1?x2−2y2=1?

Question

  1. Find a pair of positive integers, x1x1 and y1y1, that solve the equation

    (x1)2−2(y1)2=1.(x1)2−2(y1)2=1.

  2. Given integers aa, bb, we define two sequences x1x1, x2x2, x3x3, …… and y1y1, y2y2, y3y3, …… by setting

    xn+1=3xn+4yn,yn+1=axn+byn,for n≥1.xn+1=3xn+4yn,yn+1=axn+byn,for n≥1.

    Find positive values for aa, bb such that

    (xn+1)2−2(yn+1)2=(xn)2−2(yn)2.(xn+1)2−2(yn+1)2=(xn)2−2(yn)2.

  3. Find a pair of integers XX, YY which satisfy X2−2Y2=1X2−2Y2=1 such that X>Y>50X>Y>50.

  4. (Using the values of aa and bb found in the second part) what is the approximate value of xn/ynxn/yn as nn increases?
