How large is 21002100, and what is its starting digit?
Add to your resource collection

  • Solution

Question

Given that

log102=0.3010 (to 4 d.p.)log10⁡2=0.3010 (to 4 d.p.)
and that
100.2<2100.2<2
 it is possible to deduce that

  1. 21002100 begins in a 11 and is 3030 digits long;

  2. 21002100 begins in a 22 and is 3030 digits long;

  3. 21002100 begins in a 11 and is 3131 digits long;

  4. 21002100 begins in a 22 and is 3131 digits long.
