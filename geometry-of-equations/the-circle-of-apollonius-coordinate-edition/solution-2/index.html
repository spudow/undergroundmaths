Solution 2

Following on from problem 1, we have two fixed points AA and BB in the plane with AB=2aAB=2a, where a>0a>0.

A point PP moves in the plane so that

PAPB=λ,PAPB=λ,
where λ>0λ>0.

We shall now assume that λ≠1λ≠1, so that the locus of PP is a circle.

What is the length of the tangent to this circle from the mid-point of ABAB, that is, the length MNMN in the image below?

The radius and centre of the circle were found in the solution to problem 1.

This image shows the situation described in the case λ=2λ=2, where a coordinate system has been imposed and the line CNCN has been drawn in.

Incidentally, there are actually two tangents from MM to the circle, but their lengths are equal. (Can you prove this?) We have only drawn one of them.

We note first that the tangent at NN is perpendicular to the radius at NN, so the triangle CMNCMN is right-angled. We can therefore use Pythagoras’s Theorem:

CM2=CN2+MN2.CM2=CN2+MN2.

Since we know the coordinates of CC from the first part of the problem and MM is the midpoint of ABAB, we can easily work out the length CMCM. The length CNCN is the known radius of the circle, and so we can deduce the length CMCM.

We use the nice coordinates approach from the first solution to perform our calculations: AA is at (0,0)(0,0) and BB is (2a,0)(2a,0), as in the diagram above. Then CC is (2λ2aλ2−1,0)(2λ2aλ2−1,0) and the circle has radius 2λa|λ2−1|2λa|λ2−1|.

The midpoint MM has coordinates (a,0)(a,0), so
MN2=CM2−CN2=(2λ2aλ2−1−a)2−(2λa|λ2−1|)2=((λ2+1)aλ2−1)2−(2λaλ2−1)2=(λ4+2λ2+1)a2−4λ2a2(λ2−1)2=(λ4−2λ2+1)a2(λ2−1)2=a2.MN2=CM2−CN2=(2λ2aλ2−1−a)2−(2λa|λ2−1|)2=((λ2+1)aλ2−1)2−(2λaλ2−1)2=(λ4+2λ2+1)a2−4λ2a2(λ2−1)2=(λ4−2λ2+1)a2(λ2−1)2=a2.

Therefore MN=aMN=a, which is 12AB12AB, independent of the choice of λλ!

What is the locus of NN as λλ varies?

As MN=aMN=a is a constant and MM is fixed, it follows that the locus of NN as λλ varies is (part of) the circle with diameter ABAB, which is very pretty.

Which parts of this circle are included in the locus of NN as λλ varies? Are there any parts which are not?
