Double angle formula

Printable version

A double angle formula is a trigonometric identity which expresses a trigonometric function of 2θ2θ in terms of trigonometric functions of θθ. They are special cases of the compound angle formulae. The main formulae are:

cos2θsin2θtan2θ=cos2θ−sin2θ=2cos2θ−1=1−2sin2θ=2sinθcosθ=2tanθ1−tan2θcos⁡2θ=cos2⁡θ−sin2⁡θ=2cos2⁡θ−1=1−2sin2⁡θsin⁡2θ=2sin⁡θcos⁡θtan⁡2θ=2tan⁡θ1−tan2⁡θ

There are corresponding formulae for the hyperbolic functions, which can be obtained by applying Osborn’s rule to these formulae.
