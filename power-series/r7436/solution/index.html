Power Series
Review question

Can we expand (1+x)3/2+(1−2x)3/4(1+x)3/2+(1−2x)3/4 as far as the term in x3x3?

Solution

Use the binomial theorem to expand
(1+x)32+(1−2x)34(1+x)32+(1−2x)34

as far as the term in x3x3.

The binomial theorem says that, if |x|<1|x|<1, then
(1+x)r=1+rx+r(r−1)2!x2+r(r−1)(r−2)3!x3+⋯(1+x)r=1+rx+r(r−1)2!x2+r(r−1)(r−2)3!x3+⋯
 and so to expand
(1+x)32+(1−2x)34(1+x)32+(1−2x)34

as far as the term in x3x3, we can

  1. separately expand (1+x)32(1+x)32 and (1−2x)34(1−2x)34 up to x3x3 and
  2. collect the terms.
Thus
(1+x)32=1+32x+32×122!x2+32×12×(−12)3!x3+…=1+32x+38x2−348x3+…,(1+x)32=1+32x+32×122!x2+32×12×(−12)3!x3+…=1+32x+38x2−348x3+…,
 and
(1−2x)34=1+34(−2x)+34×(−14)2!(−2x)2+34×(−14)×(−54)3!(−2x)3+…=1−32x−38x2−1548x3+…,(1−2x)34=1+34(−2x)+34×(−14)2!(−2x)2+34×(−14)×(−54)3!(−2x)3+…=1−32x−38x2−1548x3+…,
 and therefore
(1+x)32+(1−2x)34=(1+32x+38x2−348x3)+(1−32x−38x2−1548x3)+…=2−1848x3+…=2−38x3+…(1+x)32+(1−2x)34=(1+32x+38x2−348x3)+(1−32x−38x2−1548x3)+…=2−1848x3+…=2−38x3+…
