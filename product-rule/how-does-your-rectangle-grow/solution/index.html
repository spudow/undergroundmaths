How does your rectangle grow?

Solution

This white rectangle has a fixed height, vv, and width uu which varies as a function of time, tt. We can write the rate of change of the width as dudtdudt or u˙u˙. You can think of this as the speed at which the right hand edge is moving.

The area of the white rectangle, A=A= u×vu×v
In a short time interval, δtδt, we can assume that u˙u˙ is roughly constant, so thinking about speed and distance…
The width increases by a small amount, δu=δu= u˙δtu˙δt
The area increases by the shaded area, δA=δA= v×u˙δtv×u˙δt
The rate of change of the area of the rectangle, dAdt≈δAδt=dAdt≈δAδt= v×u˙=vdudtv×u˙=vdudt

Note that δAδtδAδt is a fraction whereas dAdtdAdt is a derivative. They become identical in the limit as δt→0δt→0.

Our rectangle has height v=3cmv=3cm and width u=(12t2+1)cmu=(12t2+1)cm at time tsts.

What is the rate of change of area, dAdtdAdt, at

  1. t=0t=0?

  2. t=3t=3?

Differentiating, we have

dudt=12×2t=tdudt=12×2t=t
so
dAdt=vdudt=3t.dAdt=vdudt=3t.

At t=0t=0, dAdt=0dAdt=0.

At t=3t=3, dAdt=9cm2s−1dAdt=9cm2s−1.

This white rectangle has width uu and height vv, both of which vary as functions of time, tt. We can write the rates of change as u˙u˙ and v˙v˙.

The area of the white rectangle, A=A= u×vu×v
In a short time interval, δtδt, we can assume that u˙u˙ and v˙v˙ are roughly constant, so…
The width increases by δu=δu= u˙δtu˙δt
The height increases by δv=δv= v˙δtv˙δt
The area increases by approximately the shaded area, so δA≈δA≈ v×u˙δt+u×v˙δtv×u˙δt+u×v˙δt
The rate of change of the area of the rectangle, dAdt≈δAδt≈dAdt≈δAδt≈ v×u˙+u×v˙v×u˙+u×v˙
=vdudt+udvdt=vdudt+udvdt

Note that in writing down δAδA we ignored the small white rectangle in the top right-hand corner. Why is it OK to do this?

This result is known as the Product Rule which is often expressed as

ddx(uv)=udvdx+vdudx.ddx(uv)=udvdx+vdudx.

Our rectangle has v=t+1⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯√cmv=t+1cm and u=(12t2+1)cmu=(12t2+1)cm at time tsts.

What is the rate of change of area, dAdtdAdt, at

  1. t=0t=0?

  2. t=3t=3?

Differentiating, we have
dvdtdudt=12(t+1)−12=tdvdt=12(t+1)−12dudt=t

and so

dAdt=(12t2+1)×12(t+1)−12+t+1⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯√×t.dAdt=(12t2+1)×12(t+1)−12+t+1×t.

At t=0t=0, dAdt=1×12×(1)−12+0=12cm2s−1dAdt=1×12×(1)−12+0=12cm2s−1.

At t=3t=3, dAdt=(92+1)×12×(4)−12+4⎯⎯√×3=738cm2s−1dAdt=(92+1)×12×(4)−12+4×3=738cm2s−1.
