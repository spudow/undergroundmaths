Problem

Two points on the axis

Using GeoGebra, plot two points AA and BB on the xx-axis of a graph. Can you instruct GeoGebra to draw a quadratic which passes through both of them?

It should be constructed in such a way that when you move either AA or BB along the xx-axis, the quadratic changes so that it continues to pass through both of them.

If you want some guidance on how to get the coordinates of the points AA and BB, or how to enter an equation in GeoGebra, see the Using GeoGebra section.

One point as the vertex

On a new graph, plot a point AA. Can you instruct GeoGebra to draw a quadratic which has its vertex at AA?

It should be constructed in such a way that when you move AA, the quadratic changes in response.
