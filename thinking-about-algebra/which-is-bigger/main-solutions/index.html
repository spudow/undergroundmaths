Main solutions

Here’s a summary of the conclusions for each pair of fractions.

4x74x7 and 914914

When x=98x=98 the two expressions are equal. When x<98x<98 then 914914 is bigger. When x>98x>98 then 4x74x7 is bigger.

5959 and 2x122x12

When x=103x=103 the two expressions are equal. When x<103x<103 then 5959 is bigger. When x>103x>103 then 2x122x12 is bigger.

3x4+13x4+1 and x4+3x4+3

3x4+13x4+1 can be written as a single fraction: 3x+443x+44. Similarly, x4+3=x+124x4+3=x+124.

The two expressions are equal when x=4x=4. When x<4x<4, x4+3x4+3 is bigger. When x>4x>4, 3x4+13x4+1 is bigger.

8(1−x)58(1−x)5 and x6x6

The two expressions are equal when x=4853x=4853. When x<4853x<4853 then 8(1−x)58(1−x)5 is bigger. When x>4853x>4853 then x6x6 is bigger.

82x82x and 4x164x16

The two expressions are equal when x=−4x=−4 or x=4x=4. When x>4x>4, x4x4 is bigger. When 0<x<40<x<4, 82x82x is bigger. When −4<x<0−4<x<0 then x4x4 is bigger. And finally, when x<−4x<−4, 82x82x is bigger.

When x=0x=0, 82x82x is undefined so we are not able to compare the two fractions.
