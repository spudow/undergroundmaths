Students express several lengths in a unit circle diagram using trigonometric functions and thereby develop their understanding of how trigonometric functions are related to each other. This problem is accessible to any student with a basic understanding of trigonometry in right-angled triangles and offers opportunities to discuss what happens if θθ varies.
